Virat Kohli

The Run Machine of Indian Cricket

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers in the history of Indian cricket. Known for his aggressive mindset, consistency, and passion for the game, he has inspired millions of fans across the world.

Early Life

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India. From a young age, he showed immense interest in cricket and trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy.

Career Highlights

  • Former captain of the Indian cricket team
  • One of the highest run-scorers in international cricket
  • Led India to historic Test series wins overseas
  • Known for fitness and discipline

Why He Is Inspiring

Virat Kohli’s journey is a story of hard work, dedication, and self-belief. His never-give-up attitude and commitment to excellence make him a role model for young athletes.

“Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success.” – Virat Kohli
